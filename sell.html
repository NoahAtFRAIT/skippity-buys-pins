We Buy Disney Pin Collections

From vintage 90s classics to the latest WDI releases, we offer fair market value for your authentic Disney pins.

How to Sell Your Disney Pins

Deciding to part with a collection is a big decision. Whether you are clearing out space, funding a new vacation, or just moving on from the hobby, SkippityBuysPins.com is the safest place to sell. Unlike pawn shops or general resellers, we are actual Disney collectors who understand the true value of your items.

What We Buy

  • Bulk Lots: We buy "pounds" of pins for bulk pricing.
  • Limited Editions (LE): LE 100, 250, 500, etc.
  • WDI / Imagineering: Profile pins and Mickeys of Glendale exclusives.
  • DSSH / Soda Fountain: Ice cream cones, marquees, and character blocks.
  • Hidden Mickeys: Complete sets of authentic Cast Lanyard pins.

What We Don't Buy

We are proud to maintain a 5-Star Reputation in the community. To keep our inventory clean, we do not knowingly purchase:

  • "Scrappers" (Unauthorized factory seconds).
  • Fantasy Pins (Unauthorized fan art pins).
  • Damaged items (Broken posts, missing enamel).
  • Buttons or plastic souvenirs (We only buy metal enamel pins).

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from sellers like you.

How do I get a quote?

It's simple! Take a few clear photos of your pins. You don't need to photograph them individually—just lay them out on a table or snap a photo of your pin books. Email or text them to us using the buttons below.

How do you pay?

We pay immediately upon agreement and verification. We use PayPal Goods & Services (to protect both parties) or Venmo for trusted local sellers. We cover the shipping costs!

Do you buy "Fantasy" pins?

Generally, no. We specialize in official Disney trading pins (stamped with the official Disney pin trading logo on the back). However, if you have a mixed lot, we can help you sort them out.

My pins are from 2005. Are they worth more?

Age is a factor, but rarity is king! Some pins from 2005 are common "rack pins" worth a few dollars, while others are rare Limited Editions worth hundreds. Our team has the expertise to identify the "Grails" in your collection so you don't sell them for cheap.
