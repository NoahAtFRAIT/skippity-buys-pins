We Buy Disney Pin Collections

From vintage 90s classics to the latest WDI releases, we offer fair market value for your authentic Disney pins.

How to Sell Your Disney Pins

Deciding to part with a collection is a big decision. Whether you are clearing out space, funding a new vacation, or just moving on from the hobby, SkippityBuysPins.com is the safest place to sell.

What We Buy

  • Bulk Lots: We buy "pounds" of pins for bulk pricing.
  • Limited Editions (LE): LE 100, 250, 500, etc.
  • WDI / Imagineering: Profile pins and Mickeys of Glendale exclusives.
  • DSSH / Soda Fountain: Ice cream cones, marquees, and character blocks.

What We Don't Buy

To keep our inventory clean, we do not knowingly purchase:

  • "Scrappers" (Unauthorized factory seconds).
  • Fantasy Pins (Unauthorized fan art pins).
  • Damaged items (Broken posts, missing enamel).
